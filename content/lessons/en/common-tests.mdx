## Complete Blood Count (CBC)

A CBC measures several parts of your blood:

- Red blood cells - carry oxygen throughout your body
- White blood cells - fight infection
- Platelets - help your blood clot
- Hemoglobin - the protein that carries oxygen in red blood cells
- Hematocrit - the percentage of blood that's red blood cells

## Cholesterol Test

Cholesterol tests measure different types of fats in your blood:

- Total cholesterol - all cholesterol in your blood
- LDL (bad) cholesterol - can build up in arteries
- HDL (good) cholesterol - helps remove LDL from arteries
- Triglycerides - a type of fat that stores energy

:::warning
High LDL and low HDL can increase your risk of heart disease.
:::

## Blood Sugar Tests

Blood sugar tests check for diabetes or prediabetes:

- Fasting glucose - blood sugar after not eating
- A1C - average blood sugar over the past 2-3 months
- Glucose tolerance - how your body handles sugar after eating
