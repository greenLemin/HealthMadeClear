## Be Honest and Complete

Tell your doctor everything about your health, even if it feels embarrassing. Your doctor needs the full picture to help you. Don't leave anything out, including:

- All medications you take (including supplements)
- Your lifestyle habits (smoking, alcohol, exercise)
- Any changes in your health
- Your concerns and fears

## Ask for Clarification

If your doctor uses medical terms you don't understand, ask them to explain in plain language. You can say:

- "Can you explain that in simpler terms?"
- "What does that word mean?"
- "Can you write that down for me?"
- "Can you give me an example?"

## Take Notes

Write down important information during your visit, including:

- Your diagnosis (what's wrong)
- Your treatment plan
- Medication instructions
- When to follow up
- Any warning signs to watch for

:::success
It's okay to ask your doctor to slow down or repeat something you missed.
:::
