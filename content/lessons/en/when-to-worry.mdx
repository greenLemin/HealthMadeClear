## Results That Need Quick Action

Call your doctor right away if your results show:

- Very low or very high blood sugar
- Signs of infection (very high white blood cell count)
- Kidney problems (very high creatinine)
- Liver problems (very high liver enzymes)
- Severe anemia (very low hemoglobin)

:::warning
If you can't reach your doctor and feel very sick, go to urgent care or the emergency room.
:::

## Results to Monitor

Some abnormal results need attention but aren't emergencies:

- Slightly high cholesterol
- Mildly elevated blood sugar
- Slightly low or high thyroid levels
- Mild anemia

Your doctor may want to retest in a few weeks or make lifestyle changes.

## Questions to Ask Your Doctor

When discussing abnormal results, ask:

- How serious is this result?
- What could be causing it?
- Do I need more tests?
- What treatment do I need?
- How will we monitor this?
- What changes should I make?
