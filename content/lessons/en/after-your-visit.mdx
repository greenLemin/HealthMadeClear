## Review Your Instructions

As soon as you get home, review what your doctor told you. Make sure you understand:

- What medicines to take and when
- Any lifestyle changes you need to make
- When to schedule follow-up appointments
- What to do if your symptoms get worse

## Fill Your Prescriptions

If your doctor prescribed medication:

- Go to your pharmacy right away
- Show the pharmacist your insurance card
- Ask the pharmacist any questions about your medicine
- Read the label carefully before taking any medication

## Track Your Progress

Keep a health journal to track:

- Your symptoms and how they change
- Side effects from medications
- Questions that come up between visits
- Any improvements or worsening of your condition

:::info
Bring your health journal to your next appointment to help your doctor understand your progress.
:::
