## What It Is

Prognosis is a doctor's best estimate of how a disease or condition is likely to progress over time — including chances of recovery, stability, or complications. Prognosis is not a guarantee; treatment, lifestyle, and new research can change outcomes. Always ask what factors affect your personal prognosis.

## Why It Matters

Understanding this term helps you follow conversations with doctors, read labels and test results, and make informed decisions about your health.

## Talk With Your Care Team

If you are unsure how this applies to you, write down your questions before appointments and ask for explanations in plain language.
