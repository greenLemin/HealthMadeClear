## What It Is

Prognosis is a doctor's best estimate of how a disease or condition is likely to progress over time — including chances of recovery, stability, or complications. Prognosis is not a guarantee; treatment, lifestyle, and new research can change outcomes. Always ask what factors affect your personal prognosis.

## Why It Matters

Prognosis helps plan work, family, and treatment intensity — but numbers are averages, not destiny. New therapies and personal factors can shift outcomes after the first conversation.

## Talk With Your Care Team

Ask: What factors most affect my individual outlook? What would change the prognosis if I follow treatment versus if I do not?
