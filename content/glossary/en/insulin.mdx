## What It Is

Insulin is a hormone made by the pancreas that helps glucose enter cells for energy. In type 1 diabetes the body makes little or no insulin; in type 2 diabetes cells resist insulin's effects. People with diabetes may need insulin injections or other medicines to control blood sugar.

## Why It Matters

Understanding this term helps you follow conversations with doctors, read labels and test results, and make informed decisions about your health.

## Talk With Your Care Team

If you are unsure how this applies to you, write down your questions before appointments and ask for explanations in plain language.
