## What It Is

Hypotension means abnormally low blood pressure. It can cause dizziness, fainting, or fatigue, especially when standing up quickly. Causes include dehydration, certain medicines, heart problems, or severe infection. Sudden severe low blood pressure needs urgent medical care.

## Why It Matters

Low blood pressure can cause falls, especially in older adults on multiple medicines. Distinguishing harmless low readings from dangerous drops prevents injury and guides medication adjustments.

## Talk With Your Care Team

Ask: Is my low blood pressure causing my dizziness, and should any medicines be changed? When should I sit or lie down, and what hydration goals do you recommend?
