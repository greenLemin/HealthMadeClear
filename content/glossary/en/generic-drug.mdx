## What It Is

A generic drug contains the same active ingredient as a brand-name medicine and must meet the same FDA standards for safety and effectiveness. Generics often cost less because manufacturers do not repeat expensive marketing and development. They may look different but work the same way.

## Why It Matters

Generics save money and improve access for most people. Knowing they are FDA-equivalent helps you discuss switches confidently while watching for rare filler sensitivities.

## Talk With Your Care Team

Ask: Is a generic appropriate for this medicine, and will my insurance require it? What should I do if the new pill looks different or I feel different after a switch?
