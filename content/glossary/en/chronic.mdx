## What It Is

Chronic describes a condition that lasts a long time — usually three months or more. Examples include diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and heart disease. Chronic conditions often need ongoing treatment, lifestyle changes, and regular monitoring rather than a one-time cure.

## Why It Matters

Understanding this term helps you follow conversations with doctors, read labels and test results, and make informed decisions about your health.

## Talk With Your Care Team

If you are unsure how this applies to you, write down your questions before appointments and ask for explanations in plain language.
