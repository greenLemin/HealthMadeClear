## What It Is

Chronic describes a condition that lasts a long time — usually three months or more. Examples include diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and heart disease. Chronic conditions often need ongoing treatment, lifestyle changes, and regular monitoring rather than a one-time cure.

## Why It Matters

Chronic conditions require ongoing self-management, not just one visit. Understanding the label helps you plan for monitoring, medication adherence, and realistic long-term goals.

## Talk With Your Care Team

Ask: What does living with this condition look like day to day, and what are my top three self-care priorities? Who do I contact between visits if symptoms change?
