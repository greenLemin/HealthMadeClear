## What It Is

Dosage is the amount of medicine prescribed and how often you take it — for example "one tablet twice daily." Taking the correct dose at the right time is essential for safety and effectiveness. Never change your dose without talking to your doctor or pharmacist.

## Why It Matters

Wrong dose — too much, too little, or at the wrong time — is a leading cause of medication harm. Dosage instructions on the label are legal and medical orders, not suggestions.

## Talk With Your Care Team

Ask: What if I miss a dose or take one late? Can I split or crush this tablet, and should I take it with food or on an empty stomach?
