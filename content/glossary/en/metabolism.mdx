## What It Is

Metabolism is the set of chemical processes that convert food into energy and building blocks for the body. Basal metabolic rate is energy used at rest. Age, muscle mass, activity, and hormones affect metabolism. A "slow metabolism" is rarely the sole cause of weight gain.

## Why It Matters

Metabolism affects weight, energy, and how medicines are processed. Thyroid disorders, muscle mass, and age shift metabolic rate — blaming metabolism alone often misses treatable causes.

## Talk With Your Care Team

Ask: Could a thyroid or hormone issue be affecting my weight or energy? What realistic changes could help beyond dieting alone?
