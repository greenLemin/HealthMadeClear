## Question 1

Urgent care is best for:

A) Chest pain and stroke
B) Same-day non-life-threatening problems
C) Annual physical exams
D) Routine prescription refills only

answer: B
explanation: Same-day non-life-threatening problems — correct.

## Question 2

Which belongs in the ER?

A) Mild sore throat
B) Severe trouble breathing
C) Small blister
D) Routine vaccination

answer: B
explanation: Severe trouble breathing — correct.

## Question 3

What does FAST stand for in stroke warning?

A) Face, Arm, Speech, Time to call 911
B) Food, Activity, Sleep, Temperature
C) Fever, Asthma, Stress, Tiredness
D) None of the above

answer: A
explanation: Face, Arm, Speech, Time to call 911 — correct.

## Question 4

Why follow up with primary care after urgent care?

A) Not necessary
B) To coordinate ongoing treatment and records
C) Urgent care replaces your doctor forever
D) Only for children

answer: B
explanation: To coordinate ongoing treatment and records — correct.

## Question 5

When unsure about severity:

A) Wait days at home
B) Consider emergency care or call 911
C) Only use online forums
D) Skip all care

answer: B
explanation: Consider emergency care or call 911 — correct.
