## Question 1

What organ is most at risk from too much acetaminophen?

A) Heart
B) Liver
C) Kidneys
D) Lungs

answer: B
explanation: Liver — correct.

## Question 2

Why check cold medicine labels for acetaminophen?

A) It is always safe to combine
B) Multiple products can exceed daily limits
C) It only matters for children
D) Labels are optional

answer: B
explanation: Multiple products can exceed daily limits — correct.

## Question 3

When should you avoid ibuprofen without medical advice?

A) Mild headache
B) Known kidney disease or stomach ulcers
C) After exercise
D) When food is available

answer: B
explanation: Known kidney disease or stomach ulcers — correct.

## Question 4

What should you do for chest pain?

A) Take extra pain medicine and wait
B) Seek emergency care immediately
C) Only use ice packs
D) Ignore it if you are young

answer: B
explanation: Seek emergency care immediately — correct.

## Question 5

Why tell your pharmacist all medicines you take?

A) To check interactions and duplications
B) Pharmacies sell more products
C) It is not necessary for OTC drugs
D) Only for prescriptions from one doctor

answer: A
explanation: To check interactions and duplications — correct.
