## Question 1

Which of the following is an essential item for a basic first aid kit?

A) A sewing kit
B) Adhesive bandages in assorted sizes
C) A bottle of soda
D) A magazine

answer: B
explanation: Adhesive bandages are one of the most basic and essential first aid items. A well-stocked kit should include various sizes for cuts, scrapes, and blisters. They protect wounds from dirt and bacteria while they heal.

## Question 2

What is the purpose of including antiseptic wipes in a first aid kit?

A) To clean your hands before eating
B) To clean the skin around a wound to prevent infection
C) To wipe up spilled liquids
D) To remove makeup

answer: B
explanation: Antiseptic wipes kill germs on the skin around a wound, reducing the risk of infection. Clean the wound gently before applying a bandage. Do not use alcohol directly inside deep wounds, as it can damage tissue.

## Question 3

Why should you include a CPR face mask or shield in your first aid kit?

A) To protect yourself from the sun
B) To safely perform rescue breaths without direct mouth-to-mouth contact
C) To cover your face during cold weather
D) To filter dust from the air

answer: B
explanation: A CPR face mask or shield creates a barrier when giving rescue breaths, reducing the risk of disease transmission. It is a small, inexpensive item that makes bystanders more willing to perform CPR in an emergency.

## Question 4

How often should you check and restock your first aid kit?

A) Once every 10 years
B) Every few months to replace expired items and restock used supplies
C) Never, once it is stocked it lasts forever
D) Only after using it for a major emergency

answer: B
explanation: Check your first aid kit every few months. Replace expired medications, restock used bandages and wipes, and make sure the flashlight batteries work. A kit that is out of date or missing supplies is not helpful in an emergency.

## Question 5

Where is the best place to store a first aid kit in your home?

A) In a locked safe that only adults can open
B) In an easily accessible location that everyone in the household knows about
C) In the garage behind boxes
D) In a high cabinet that requires a step stool to reach

answer: B
explanation: Your first aid kit should be easy to reach in an emergency. Place it in a central, accessible location and make sure all household members know where it is. Avoid locking it, as seconds matter during an injury.
