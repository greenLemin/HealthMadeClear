## Question 1

Obstructive sleep apnea is caused by:

A) Dreaming too much
B) Blocked airflow when throat muscles relax
C) Too much caffeine only
D) Normal snoring always

answer: B
explanation: Blocked airflow when throat muscles relax — correct.

## Question 2

A reason to seek evaluation:

A) Snoring plus daytime sleepiness
B) One night of poor sleep
C) Occasional yawn
D) Reading before bed

answer: A
explanation: Snoring plus daytime sleepiness — correct.

## Question 3

CPAP works by:

A) Surgery on the throat
B) Delivering air pressure to keep the airway open
C) Replacing all exercise
D) Stopping breathing on purpose

answer: B
explanation: Delivering air pressure to keep the airway open — correct.

## Question 4

Untreated sleep apnea increases risk of:

A) Only dry skin
B) High blood pressure and heart disease among others
C) Improved driving
D) Lower weight always

answer: B
explanation: High blood pressure and heart disease among others — correct.

## Question 5

If CPAP is uncomfortable:

A) Stop forever without telling anyone
B) Ask a sleep specialist about mask fit and alternatives
C) Drive long trips while drowsy
D) Only use it once a month

answer: B
explanation: Ask a sleep specialist about mask fit and alternatives — correct.
